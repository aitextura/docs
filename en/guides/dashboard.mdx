## Time Period Selector

At the top of the screen, under the **Analytics** heading, you will find tabs for selecting a time range:

| Period | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **6 months** | Half-year summary (selected by default) |
| **3 months** | Quarterly trends |
| **30 days** | Monthly overview |
| **7 days** | Weekly activity |
| **24 hours** | Data for the last day |

Simply click the desired tab — all metrics and the chart will instantly update.

---

## Metric Cards

Below the period selector, there are four clickable cards with key indicators:

| Metric | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Message Cost** | Total message cost in dollars for the selected period |
| **Messages** | Total number of messages processed by all agents |
| **Chats** | Number of unique conversations |
| **FAQ** | Number of questions that matched the FAQ section |

Each card displays a **percentage change** compared to the previous equivalent period. For example, +600% means a sixfold increase.

<Tip>
  Click any card — the chart below will update to show the trend for that specific metric.
</Tip>

---

## Line Chart

Below the cards is a line chart that visualizes the selected metric over time. The horizontal axis shows dates, the vertical axis shows values. The chart automatically adjusts to the selected period and metric.

---

## Balance and Words

In the top-right corner of the screen (and in the sidebar), you can always see:

- **Balance** — your current account balance in dollars
- **Word count** — remaining word limit
- **Top Up** button — for quickly adding funds

<Warning>
  Your balance is visible on every page of the platform — keep an eye on it so your agents don't stop due to insufficient funds.
</Warning>

## How to Use the Dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a time period">
    Click the desired tab at the top of the screen: **24 hours**, **7 days**, **30 days**, **3 months**, or **6 months**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the metric cards">
    Look at the four cards: message cost, number of messages, chats, and FAQ. Pay attention to the percentage change indicators.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the metric you are interested in">
    Click a card to update the line chart and display the trend for the selected indicator.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your balance">
    In the top-right corner, make sure you have sufficient funds. If needed, click **Top Up**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is the dashboard data updated in real time?">
    Yes, every time you visit the page you see the most current data.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I view analytics for a specific employee?">
    The dashboard shows a summary across all agents. For detailed statistics on a specific employee, use the [Billing History](/en/guides/finance) section.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What does the percentage next to a metric mean?">
    It represents the change compared to the previous equivalent period. If "30 days" is selected, the comparison is with the preceding 30 days.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
